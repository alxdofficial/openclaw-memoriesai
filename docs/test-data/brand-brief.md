# Surge Energy — Brand Brief (March 2025)

## Product
Natural energy drink, 250ml cans.
Key ingredients: green tea extract (150mg natural caffeine), lion's mane mushroom, B vitamins, no artificial sweeteners or flavourings.
Flagship SKU: SE-ORIGINAL, SE-CITRUS, SE-BERRY.

## Pricing
- Single can: £2.49 (retail)
- 12-pack: £27.99
- 12-pack subscription: £22.99/month
- Average order value: £28.50

## Target Audience
18–25 UK students and young professionals. Fitness-minded but not hardcore. Tired of Red Bull/Monster. Values clean ingredients. Studies hard, trains occasionally, works long shifts.

## Brand Tone
No-BS, functional, clean. Not extreme sports. Not corporate wellness. Just real energy for real days.

## Platforms
TikTok (primary), Instagram Reels (secondary).

## Content Pillars
1. Morning routine / productivity — "how I start my day"
2. Study sessions — desk setups, late-night library content
3. Pre/post gym — not performance obsessed, just consistent
4. Taste/flavour content — honest reviews, recipe hacks

## What Works
- "Day in my life" featuring Surge naturally (not forced)
- "Study with me" background content with can visible
- Gym vlog with Surge as part of the routine
- Before/after energy comparisons (coffee vs Surge)

## What to Avoid
- No direct health claims (UK ASA rules)
- No extreme sports or high-risk activities
- No direct competitor mentions
- No overly produced/fake content — authentic only
